Game Instructions

  1. Drag your cursor around the Etch-a-Sketch to create fun pictures!

  2. Click "Shake!" to erase the Etch-a-Sketch.

  3. Change the resolution on the Etch-a-Sketch for fun!

  4. Click "Advanced" to draw in greyscale!

  5. Click the "Crazy!" button and see what happens!
